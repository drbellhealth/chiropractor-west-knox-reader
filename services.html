Chiropractic Services in Knoxville

Here's what they actually do. The adjustment is the main thing. Everything else helps it hold, so you come back less. Short list. Real work.

  • Soft-tissue therapy and trigger-point work

  • Auto-accident and personal-injury evaluations

Adjustment & Manual Therapy

The adjustment is the core. A quick, careful push to one joint to get it moving. It's fast. It barely hurts. The pop you hear is just gas leaving the joint. Nothing scary. For most west-siders, a few of these clears the problem and gets you back to normal.

Spinal Decompression & Traction

Decompression uses a motorized table. It gently stretches your spine. That takes pressure off your discs and nerves. It helps with sciatica, a bulging or herniated disc, and lower back pain that won't quit. It's usually a multi-week program. And it's a conservative thing people try before surgery ever comes up.

Soft-Tissue & Rehabilitation

Soft-tissue work is the muscle stuff. Trigger points. Myofascial release. A little hand tool. It usually comes with your adjustment, because tight muscles pull joints back out of place. Common out here for desk folks and weekend athletes alike.

Wellness & Maintenance Care

Once you're better, some people keep coming in once in a while. That's optional. It makes sense if you sit all day, train hard, or hurt your back badly before. You can skip it otherwise. A good office says maintenance is your choice, not a rule.

What a First Visit Looks Like

Your first visit takes about 45 minutes to an hour. They take your history. They run a focused exam. They check how you move. Then they tell you what they found and the plan.
