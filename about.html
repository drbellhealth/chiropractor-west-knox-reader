About West Knoxville Chiropractor, Explained

An independent local guide to chiropractic care in West Knoxville, Tennessee, with a focus on Bell Family Chiropractic on Sherlake Lane.

What This Site Is

This site exists to help people on the west side of Knoxville understand local chiropractic care — what an office treats, what a first visit looks like, which neighborhoods are served, and how to choose a steady local practice — before they call. The information is general and educational and isn't a substitute for an in-person evaluation.

About the Practice We Cover

Bell Family Chiropractic is a family office in West Knoxville. The care is hands-on and conservative: adjustments, spinal decompression, soft-tissue work. The goal is simple. Get you back to normal without piling on scans, pills, or surgery. They see all ages. They take major insurance. They handle car-accident and work-injury cases too. Want the details? Check the West Knoxville chiropractor on Sherlake Lane. The office is located at 111 Sherlake Lane, Suite 101, Knoxville, TN 37922, and the main number is +1 865-383-7730.
